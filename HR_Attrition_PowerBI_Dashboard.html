KEY INSIGHTS & RECOMMENDATIONS
📌
INSIGHT 1 — Overall Attrition
30.2% overall attrition rate — 151 out of 500 employees left. Industry benchmark is typically 10-15%, so this is 2x the healthy rate.
📌
INSIGHT 2 — Department Risk
HR (36.2%) and Sales (35.3%) are the highest-risk departments and need immediate attention from management.
📌
INSIGHT 3 — Overtime Impact
Overtime employees leave at 52.5% vs 15.6% for non-overtime staff — that is 3x higher risk. This is the strongest single predictor of attrition.
📌
INSIGHT 4 — Salary Gap
Employees earning below ₹30K have 83.3% attrition rate. This is critical — almost every low-salary employee leaves.
📌
INSIGHT 5 — Job Satisfaction
Score 1 satisfaction → 52.5% attrition. Score 4 satisfaction → only 16.9%. Satisfaction is a strong lever for retention.
📌
INSIGHT 6 — Young Talent
Age group 18-25 has 47.4% attrition — the highest among all age groups. Young employees need better career growth paths.
✅
REC 1 — Salary Review
Immediately review and increase salaries for the Below ₹30K bracket. This single action can drastically reduce attrition.
✅
REC 2 — Overtime Policy
Limit overtime to 2 days/week max or provide compensation bonuses. Create a mandatory rest policy.
✅
REC 3 — Young Talent Retention
Create mentorship programs, learning budgets, and career roadmaps specifically for the 18-35 age group.
✅
REC 4 — Satisfaction Surveys
Conduct quarterly job satisfaction surveys. Assign HR action owners to all teams with satisfaction score below 2.
